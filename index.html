A Modern Resident Market

Open the cooler. Grab what you need. Keep moving.

Seattle Godega uses a modern refrigerated smart-market setup designed for high-capacity drinks, snacks, fresh options, and everyday essentials. Residents can make quick self-service purchases without waiting in line or leaving the building.

Refrigerated

Great for energy drinks, sparkling water, protein shakes, cold brew, and grab-and-go items.

High capacity

Designed for larger product variety than a traditional coil vending setup.

Cashless payments

Simple card-reader checkout experience for residents.

Remote management

Inventory, sales data, and restocking needs can be managed without manual guesswork.

How It Works

Simple for property teams. Useful for residents.

01

We place the market

We work with your team to identify a secure, visible location in your building, such as a lobby, package area, lounge, or amenity space.

02

We stock & maintain it

Seattle Godega handles product selection, restocking, pricing, maintenance, and resident support.

03

Residents grab & go

Residents get convenient 24/7 access to quick purchases without a trip outside the building.

About The Founder

Local, personal, and community-focused.

Seattle Godega is a Hispanic-owned Seattle small business founded by Joseph Ayala. Inspired by the neighborhood bodega concept, Seattle Godega aims to bring convenient access to everyday essentials directly into the communities where people live.
